First Enemy Creation
Create an enemy that does the following:
- Patrols around areas.
- On players sight he:
- Targets a random player.
- Follows him.
- Once he is 200m away, he finds the best spot to attack.
- Moves to the attack spot.
- Once in the attack spot, dashes towards the player to punch them. 
Nice to haves:
- He can dodge bullets if the player plans on shooting at him.
